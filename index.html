While studying at Flinders University, I helped run a couple of clubs, and did some graphic design work for these clubs. This ranged across digitial designs (e.g. logos, social media, TV slides, event posters, etc.), physical branding (vinyl banners, decals, stickers, coreflute signage, etc.) and merchandise (embroidery, screen printing, and POD merch), as well as other bits and pieces (like painting a mural). This site holds a collection of various works I've made. The designs here are displayed in no particular order, and you can click on each to view a brief blurb of info (once you click on one you can also use the arrows to step through them all in order). Currently, all of the designs here were made for Flinders Academic Science Club (FLASC) or the Flinders University Computer Society (FUCS).

FLASC Discord Logo 2025

A logo for a 'Flinders Science' discord server, covering chemistry, physics, biology, and more.

The science department at Flinders is centred around a courtyard known as Anchor Court, which has a big anchor in the middle, so I used that idea for the logo, rather than favouring any one discipline or trying to display all disciplines at once.
